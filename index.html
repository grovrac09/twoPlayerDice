How to play:

  • Player 1 rolls first, and can roll as many times as they want.
  • A player can hold at any point to save their score, but they can't roll again unless the next player holds.
  • If a player rolls the number 1, their score will reset, and the next player rolls.
  • The first player to score 20 or higher wins.
